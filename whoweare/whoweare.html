WHO WE ARE





Hack for Sweden is Sweden's largest yearly hackaton organized by government agencies. It's focus is to stimulate and broaden the use of open data for the benefit of society. The hackaton of 2018 is the fifth since the beginning in 2014. The main responsibility has rotated each year between the agencies, this year Arbetsförmedlingen is the head organizer. Previous agencies have been SMHI, Tillväxtverket, Naturvårdsverket and Sveriges Geologiska Undersökning, SGU.

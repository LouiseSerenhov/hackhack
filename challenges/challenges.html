Challenges


The challenges of the hackathon, Hack for Sweden 2018, have been produced in cooperation with our partner government agencies. The challenges are clustered under the following six categories. The specific challenges under each category will be presented here, prior to the event. Each week up until the hackathon weekend, 13-15 April, we will reveal the specific challenges for one of the categories. Keep posted and follow us on social media!

May The Force Be With You

    Please note that these are only teaser descriptions of the complete challenges in the category. The challenges will be presented in their whole glory when we come closer to D day. Patience is a virtue!


  • The force is strong with this one
    Create a solution that enables Police officers and Security Guards to cooperate and interact smoothly, in order to optimize service to citizens and society.


  • In a dark place we find ourselves, and a little more knowledge lights our way
    Create a solution for public protection and disaster relief personnel (PPDR personnel), that increases their safety, accuracy and ability to make informed decisions. Depending on the context, solutions could include shared and crowdsourced data, AI, geo-mapping, simulations and visualizations.


  • Adventure. Excitement. A Jedi craves not these things
    Create a solution for PPDR personnel to act faster on and more efficiently handle the possible damage of a disaster or terrorist attack - for example poisoned or polluted drinking water.


How You Doin' Sweden

    Please note that these are only teaser descriptions of the complete challenges in the category. The challenges will be presented in their whole glory when we come closer to D day. Patience is a virtue!


  • Tomorrow never dies
    Create a solution that helps government agencies and other parties gain insights by visualizing the correlation between the state of health of the population and their access to health beneficial factors within their region. How many data sources can you juggle?


  • For your eyes only
    Create a solution that allows children and youths to crowdsource data in order to gather a current view of their mental health status, for government agencies to act accordingly. Can gamification be a clever tool?


  • The world is not enough
    Create a solution to engage the population of Sweden to contribute to the conservation and enrichment of our cultural heritage. Find and document our cultural heritage in order to maintain it for the benefit of future generations!

I Like The Way You Move

    Please note that these are only teaser descriptions of the complete challenges in the category. The challenges will be presented in their whole glory when we come closer to D day. Patience is a virtue!


  • West side story
    Create a solution that presents relevant spots of interest (e.g. nature scenery, historical landmarks, cultural heritage) in the proximity according to interest, encouraging exploration of the area in new ways.


  • Flashdance
    Create a solution that fully utilizes the new possibilities of tech. Use all the resources available to bring places and objects to life for those that can’t play an active part in community life, and allow exploration from a distance


  • Saturday night fever
    Create a solution that optimizes the travel routes for PPDR (public protection and disaster relief) personnel when trying to navigate and localise a citizen in distress. Explore how tech can facilitate effective communication.

Smells Like Green Spirit

    Please note that these are only teaser descriptions of the complete challenges in the category. The challenges will be presented in their whole glory when we come closer to D day. Patience is a virtue!


  • Take a walk on the wild side
    Create a solution that triggers citizens to explore nature areas and wildlife through gamification!


  • Bridge over troubled water
    Create a solution that facilitates for citizens engaging in recreational fishing and hunting, by providing necessary and relevant contextual information.


  • Blowing in the wind
    Create a solution that helps citizens become aware of their Co2 footprint in daily life, and when travelling. The solution should also provide information and suggestions on how to climate compensate accordingly.

AI have a dream

    Please note that these are only teaser descriptions of the complete challenges in the category. The challenges will be presented in their whole glory when we come closer to D day. Patience is a virtue!


  • If not us, who? If not now, when?
    Create a solution that provides data driven suggestions on how to best utilise the available cropland in order to optimise sustainable strategic thinking. What should we produce, and where? How self sufficient can we become?


  • Who seeks shall find
    Create a solution that facilitates for newly arrived to explore what Sweden has to offer tailored to their work experience, competence (hard skills and soft skills) and preferences. How can newly arrived find the best suitable spot to work and live, and how can they find out where in Sweden their competence and worklife experience is most needed?


  • In the middle of difficulty lies opportunity
    Everybody needs help with making their competencies and skills visible, regardless of language, age and experience! Create a solution that puts matching the skills and competences of individuals to jobs in focus - minimizing bias and discrimination on the Swedish labour market.

Rythm Of The Algorythm

    Please note that these are only teaser descriptions of the complete challenges in the category. The challenges will be presented in their whole glory when we come closer to D day. Patience is a virtue!


  • Wild Card
    Let your imagination flow! Do you have a challenge of your own? Go for it!


  • Holy indexation, Batman!
    Create a solution that allows for a natural language processing (NLP) based search, in several open data resources simultaneously. Let’s make open data truly accessible and open!


  • Red pill or blue pill
    Create a contribution to the digital democracy arena in Sweden! Help make participative civic tech the next big thing by bringing your best to the co-creational table!
